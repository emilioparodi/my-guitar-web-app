ABOUT THE ARTIST

ARTISTIC CAREER

Guitarist and composer, Emilio Parodi Garcia for years plays concerts in theaters, and art events.
Graduated in Italy at Conservatory G.Lettimi in Rimini in Classical Guitar with master degree (2012).
on live presents their composition and pieces by famous authors, trying to engage the public in an atmosphere typically "Andina".
As Guitarist actually he works as Teacher, Concert player and Composer qualified in using software like Logic Studio, Musescore, Finale and Guitar Pro.

MUSIC EXPERIENCE

In San Marino & Italy's Music Intitutes Emilio receive educational guitar classes by Paolo Santi , Maurizio Cerqua and received Master guitar classes by Michelangelo Severi.
In 2008 he joined the faculty of Bellas Artes, University of Quindio (Colombia) under the guidance of Javier Mauricio Carvajal Mejia.
He participated in the Bach Marathon in Rimini (2012), performed a solo concert in the Theater Music & Región of Armenia in Colombia (2013), has performed to the street artists event S.M.I.A.F. in San Marino and joined the International Classical Guitar Competition organized by the Forum Guitar in Wien (2015) where received Masterclasses by Olaf Van Gonnissen and Andrea de Vitis.
He has attended specific classical guitar teaching courses managed by Stefano Viola (2016) and Pietro Bonaguri (2017). Recently he did a classical guitar improvement course at Segovia Guitar Academy with Josué Gutiérrez (2022).

TEACHER

Emilio has teached classical and electric guitar at The Music Workshop in Wien (2015) and made Propedeutical Music Courses for the Associazione G. Lettimi in Rimini (2016-2022).
Since 2016 he has been providing music substitute teaching at the Istituto Musicale Sammarinese and and the San Marino Secondary School.
