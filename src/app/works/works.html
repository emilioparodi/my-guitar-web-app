HORMIGAS, comida para guitarra

This guitar works comes from my insects passion and title (Ants, guitar food) explain what I mean. I imagine to transform my guitar in an Anthill and see how these insects works and how they organize their life in this instrument, trying to understand what the appropriate music I can play in that moment.

MELODIE ÜBER DEN FLUSS

As a musician I’m really happy to have lived and worked in Vienna, a 360° musical city and a marvelous European attraction. In this city I was able to have a full immersion in what my guitar studies were like and was precious for expand my music style. The solo, like title and image explains, is inspired by a sensational attraction of this city: the Danube.

FURTO A REGOLA D'ARTE

Composed in two movements: Il colpo and Piano B, represents an invented story about a four gangsters reunited in an old basement to organize the perfect heist of their life. So the boss start to explain the plan and here comes the music, which will help us to imagine the story. And for all best plans there is a second emergency plan called “Piano B”.

SUITE COLOMBIANA

A trip is music. It teaches us to know ourselves better, leads us to face unpredictable situations, and above all, fills us with overwhelming emotions. After completing my Conservatory studies, I wanted to embark on this wonderful experience in Colombia, the land where I was born and that I could not live through a series of events. "Suite Colombiana" is a collection of colors, emotions and moments spent knowing this land rich in musicality, and smiles. Each song tells a different story, and all together represent my experience, my first solo guitar work.
