Privacy & Cookie Policy

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how the website emilioguitar.club is managed, with reference to the processing of personal data of users who visit it and use the available services.

2. Data Collected

While browsing the site, the following data may be collected:

  • Navigation data: IP address, browser type, operating system, visited pages (collected anonymously for statistical purposes).
  • Data voluntarily provided by the user: information entered in the contact form (name, email, message) managed through the service Formspree.

3. Purpose of Processing

The data collected is used for the following purposes:

  • Responding to requests submitted via the contact form;
  • Monitoring site traffic and improving content through analytics tools (e.g., Google Analytics);
  • Ensuring the security of the site and the data transmitted.

4. Cookies

The site uses technical cookies and may use analytical and third-party cookies (e.g., Google Analytics) to collect aggregated and anonymous information about site usage.

You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.

5. Google Analytics

This site will use Google Analytics, a web analytics service provided by Google Inc. Google uses the collected data to track and examine the use of the site, compile reports, and share them with other Google services.

Users can opt out of Google Analytics data collection by installing the browser add-on available at the following link: Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

6. User Rights

Users have the right to:

  • Request access to their personal data;
  • Request correction or deletion of their data;
  • Object to the processing of their data for legitimate reasons.

To exercise these rights, please contact us here: Contacts.

7. Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated at any time. Changes will be published on this page.
